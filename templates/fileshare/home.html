What This Site Does

Parafiles helps invited creators upload mod packages, organize files into folders, and share short public download links. Public visitors can download shared files, check basic metadata, and report files or folders that appear unsafe, unlawful, or infringing.

How It Is Run

The service is operated as a small EU-hosted project. Accounts are private and invitation-only, uploads may be scanned or reviewed for abuse, and files are hosted as available rather than as permanent storage.

Before Using It

Only upload files you have the legal right to distribute. Do not upload paid mod reuploads, stolen creator assets, leaked builds, malware, sensitive personal data, or anything that violates applicable law.
